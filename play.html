Web QnA is an innovative online question and answer platform that I had the privilege to lead from its inception. Initially a proof of concept, it now boasts 2 million daily users. Leveraging Next.js and server-side rendering, Web QnA's optimized pages rank exceptionally well in Google searches, reaching the top 3 on average. Its purpose is to aid students in finding academic solutions, making it accessible even in areas with slow internet. Leading the project, I managed a proficient team, ensuring scalability, robustness, and user satisfaction, showcasing my adeptness in project management and cutting-edge technologies. Web QnA stands as a testament to my commitment to impactful digital solutions.

This innovative platform enables students to learn and practice their skills within the context of their academic grade and textbooks through the unique implementation of GuardRails. Byjus Wiz represents a pioneering application of AI in education, fostering targeted and dynamic learning interactions. Infosys Meridian (https://www.infosys.com/products-and-platforms/meridian.html)

Infosys Meridian is an innovative online VR collaborative platform developed in response to the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, addressing the limitations of conventional video conferencing and remote interactions. This cutting-edge solution provides an immersive VR experience, enabling users to feel physically present in virtual venues alongside other participants. By offering a more engaging and interactive environment, Meridian successfully bridges the gap between remote interactions and in-person meetings. Its popularity has led to widespread adoption both within Infosys and among its clients, extending its usage even beyond the pandemic era.
